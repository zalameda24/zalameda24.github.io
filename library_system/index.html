What is Responsible AI Use?

AI is transforming the Philippines by driving progress in economy, governance, sustainable development, and human well-being. When used responsibly, AI benefits all communities while respecting cultural values and supporting marginalized groups.

Transparency and Disclosure

AI systems must have clear privacy policies that inform users about data collection and consent. Users need to understand how AI works and the potential consequences of its use.

  • Clear privacy policies for all users
  • Transparent consent mechanisms
  • Easy-to-understand documentation
  • Disclosure of data usage practices

Accuracy and Accountability

All individuals, groups, universities, and institutions must be responsible for AI use and held accountable for its consequences. This ensures ethical deployment across all sectors.

  • Clear accountability frameworks
  • Institutional responsibility
  • Consequence management systems
  • Regular audits and reviews

Ethical AI and Bias Mitigation

AI systems must be assessed for effectiveness in reducing gender bias and discrimination. Preventive measures should be implemented in data models and algorithms to ensure fairness.

  • Gender bias assessment
  • Non-discrimination standards
  • Fair algorithm design
  • Continuous bias monitoring

Legal Compliance and IP Rights

AI must align with Philippine laws including the Data Privacy Act and Cybercrime Prevention Act. Human-created work with AI assistance is protected under the Intellectual Property Code.

  • Data Privacy Act compliance
  • Cybercrime Prevention Act adherence
  • IP protection for human-AI creations
  • National security protection
